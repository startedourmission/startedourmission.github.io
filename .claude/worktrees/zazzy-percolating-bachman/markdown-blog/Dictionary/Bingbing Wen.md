University of Washington Information School 박사과정생으로 [[Lucy Lu Wang]]의 지도를 받고 있습니다. LLM abstention(모델이 불확실하거나 답할 수 없는 질문에 답변을 거부하는 능력)을 집중 연구합니다.

- "Characterizing LLM Abstention Behavior in Science QA with Context Perturbations" (EMNLP Findings 2024) - 과학 QA에서의 abstention 행동 분석
- "MARVEL: Modular Abstention for Reliable and Versatile Expert LLMs" (ICML 2025 Workshop)
- "Clarify or Answer: Reinforcement Learning for Agentic VQA with Context Under-specification" (arXiv:2601.16400, 2026)
- "Agentic Abstention: Do Agents Know When to Stop Instead of Act?" (arXiv:2606.28733, 2026)
